INDUSTRIES SERVED BY PARAKH

Education has been difficult to access after the pandemic. We always believe education must not be a second priority in any circumstances. With our 10+ years of experience in the education sector, we have created various products, specifically for education industries i.e. schools, colleges, universities, coaching centers, tuition centres to transform their traditional education into a digital classroom.




The examination industry has changed significantly with the innovation of technology. With the rise of demand in education in our country, examination has also become an important part of the system. With our 10+ years of experience in serving the examination Industry, we have understood the pain areas of the examination industry and developed various innovative products to solve the problems of conducting examinations for educational institutes, government bodies, and corporates.

BRANDS OWNED BY PARAKH

Exampanda offers fully equipped exam venues with the latest computer system, security cameras, and biometric solutions for different exams across India.

EduVue is a complete institute management solution for education institutes, i.e., schools, colleges, universities, coaching centers, tuition centers in India.

Book Infra is an all-in-one online portal for searching, and booking exam venues in a centralized way. BookInfra also provides ERP solutions for exam venues and exam venue setup consultancy services for entrepreneurs.
